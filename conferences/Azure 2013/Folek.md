Cloud
===
Based on 4 premises:
 - Pooled resources
 - self-service
 - elasticity
 - based on usage
    - the user manages the cloud devices in terms of hardware parameters, location etc.

Focus goes to virtualization and externalizing platforms and infrastructures to the cloud.
